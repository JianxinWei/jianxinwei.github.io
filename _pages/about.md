## Personal Introduction


I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI) in the UAE, working with <a href="https://tingyu-tyu.github.io/" target="_blank">Prof. Ting Yu</a>. My research focuses on privacy-preserving machine learning algorithms and synthetic data generation. Previously, I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the National University of Singapore (NUS) in 2025, where I was advised by <a href="https://www.comp.nus.edu.sg/~xiaoxk/index.html" target="_blank">Prof. Xiaokui Xiao</a>. I obtained my B.Eng. in Information Security from the School of Computer Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU).
